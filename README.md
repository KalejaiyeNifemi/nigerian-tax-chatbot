# Nigerian Tax Law Chatbot 🇳🇬

A RAG-based AI chatbot that answers questions about Nigerian tax laws,
built on Google Gemini, LangChain, ChromaDB, and FastAPI.

## Problem Statement
Navigating Nigerian tax legislation is complex and inaccessible to most
individuals and small business owners. This chatbot provides instant,
document-grounded answers to tax questions using Retrieval Augmented
Generation (RAG).

## Data Sources
- Federal Inland Revenue Service (firs.gov.ng)
- Companies Income Tax Act (CITA)
- Personal Income Tax Act (PITA)
- Value Added Tax Act (VATA)
- Finance Acts 2020–2023
